What Pet Taxi Offers

Pet Taxi provides transportation services specially designed to meet the needs of pets. Our vehicles ensure a comfortable and safe environment for both the animal and the owner. Each service is created to make the journey as calm and predictable as possible for you and your pet.

Transportation with the owner: The vehicles are adapted to provide comfort for both the pet and the owner during the trip. Up to 4 pets can be accommodated depending on their size.

Read more

Transportation without the owner: We pick up the pet, transport it to the necessary clinic or care center, and wait if needed for the return trip.

Why Was Pet Taxi Created?

Not everyone is ready to accept a pet in their vehicle. But your dog, cat, or guinea pig should not suffer because of that. We have heard the stories—declined bookings, dissatisfied drivers, uncomfortable trips.
Pet Taxi was created because of exactly these stories.
So that these stories no longer exist. So that when you cannot accompany your pet, we can do it for you—with care, respect, and calm. Our service offers tailored transportation, caring drivers, and an environment created for the comfort, safety, and peace of your pet. We are here to make every journey as smooth and pleasant as possible for both you and your pet.
